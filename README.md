# The SoftwareInterviewSupportSuite
This is a folder for various projects in the software engineer support suite: a collection of projects that I am making to track my journey to getting into FAANG++.  I am targetting the original Acronym plus a few other big players in the data/engineering/fintech spaces. (Ramp, Plaid, Databricks, Palantir, Cloudflare and Microsoft for example)

The subprojects (dare I say microservices?) are intended to be run locally, as needed, with a data/json-api.

# The Services:
Leetcode Tracker - A tracker for all attempts on Leetcode interviews and problems.
    - Stores your solutions
    - Tracks attempts
    - Tracks metadata on the question
    - Tracks write ups and understandings of each attempt
    - Provides Spaced repetition for problems so you dont forget them
    - Tracks where you have seen (ie. which companies) certain problems and when
    - Lets you create lists with subsections and track your progress towards completing that list

Resume, Application and Interview Tracker - A datastore for all resume's you have sent, who they have been sent to, whether they got you an interview or not, how far in the interview cycle you went and any additional information like offers, notes, feedback and personal impressions

Resume Generator - Give an llm your tasks and job description and watch it generate your Work Experience and Projects section

Mock Interview Generator - Chooses two LC questions from a pool of Mediums and Hards and One System Design Question from a pool of HelloInterview and System Design Interview question and give you a timeline to work under.  When you are done, mark which questions you succeeded and which you failed and why.  Saves this and adds it to the spaced repetition.

Habit Tracker - Give it a range and it will return, by day, your "events" (Applications sent and LC problems solved)
